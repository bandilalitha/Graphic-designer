Consultative Approach

    
                
 
                    with a consultative approach the designer takes on a more advisory role .They 
                    utilize their expertise and experience to guide the client through the design
                    process.The desigher offers suggestions,recommendations,and alternatives based
                    on their knowledge of design principles,industry trends,and the client's 
                    requirements.The final decisions rest with the client,but they rely on the 
                    designer's professional insights.

...02

Itrative Approach

                

                    In an iterative approach the designer emphasizes the 
                   importance of continuous improvement and refinement. 
                   They present initial design concepts to the clent 
                   and actively seek feedback. Based on the client's 
                   input, the designer makes adjustments and further 
                   develops the design, repeating this cycle until the 
                   client is satisfied with the final outcome.

...03

Adaptive Approach

                

                    An adaptive designer pays close attention to the client's 
                    personality,style,and preferences.They adapt their design 
                    process and communication style to align with the client's needs.
                    This approach requires the designer to be versatile,fexible,and 
                    capable of adjusting their methods and ideas to match the client's 
                    expectations effectively.

...04

Educative Approach

                
 
                    In this approach, the designer takes on the role of an educator 
                    enlightening and client about design principles,trends,and 
                    possibilities. The designer helps the client understand the 
                    rationale behind certain design choices and encourages them to 
                    explore innovative ideas. They aim to empower the client with 
                    knowledge and insights to make informed decisions throughout the 
                    design process.
